Getting Started

What is this service?

This is a personal SMS notification service operated by Raghuveer Ganta that sends automated trading alerts to your mobile phone. You'll receive real-time notifications about trade executions, position updates, and market signals from platforms like NinjaTrader and TradingView.

How do I sign up?

To sign up:

  1. Visit our home page
  2. Enter your mobile phone number
  3. Check the consent checkbox (it's unchecked by default)
  4. Click "Register for Alerts"

You'll start receiving alerts once your registration is processed.

Is there a cost to subscribe?

There is no subscription fee for this service. However, standard message and data rates from your mobile carrier may apply. Check with your carrier about your texting plan.

What phone carriers are supported?

The service works with all major U.S. carriers including AT&T, T-Mobile, Verizon, Sprint, and most smaller carriers. If you're unsure, contact us at raghustocks2020@gmail.com.

About Messages

How many messages will I receive?

Message frequency varies based on trading activity:

  • Average: 10-30 messages per day during active trading hours
  • Peak periods: Higher volume during volatile markets
  • Quiet periods: Few or no messages during market closures, weekends, holidays

What types of messages will I receive?

You'll receive notifications about:

  • Trade executions (buy/sell orders filled)
  • Position updates (P&L changes, position size)
  • Trading signals from automated strategies
  • System alerts (connectivity issues, errors)

Important: These are personal trading notifications ONLY. We never send marketing messages, promotions, or advertisements.

When will I receive messages?

Messages are sent in real-time when trading events occur, typically during:

  • Stock market hours: 9:30 AM - 4:00 PM ET
  • Futures market hours: 6:00 PM - 5:00 PM ET (Sunday-Friday)
  • Pre-market and after-hours trading sessions

Are these messages financial advice?

No. These messages are personal trading notifications only. They do NOT constitute financial advice, investment recommendations, or solicitations to buy or sell securities. All trading decisions are made independently.

Opting Out & Managing Subscription

How do I unsubscribe?

To stop receiving messages, simply reply with STOP to any message. You can also text:

  • UNSUBSCRIBE
  • CANCEL
  • QUIT

Your opt-out request will be processed within seconds, and you'll receive a confirmation message.

Is there a fee to opt-out?

No. Opting out is completely free with no penalties or consequences.

Can I re-subscribe after opting out?

Yes. If you opt-out and later want to re-subscribe, visit our home page and register again with your phone number.

How do I get help?

For assistance, you can:

  • Reply HELP to any message for instant help information
  • Email raghustocks2020@gmail.com
  • Visit our Contact page

Technical Issues

I'm not receiving messages. What should I do?

If you're not receiving messages, check the following:

  1. Verify you successfully completed registration
  2. Make sure you haven't accidentally replied STOP
  3. Check if your phone has blocked the number
  4. Ensure you have cellular signal
  5. Contact your carrier to verify SMS is enabled

If problems persist, email raghustocks2020@gmail.com with your phone number.

Messages are delayed. Why?

Message delays are typically caused by:

  • Carrier network congestion
  • Poor cellular signal
  • Phone in low-power mode

While we send messages instantly, carrier delivery times may vary. This is outside our control.

Can I change my phone number?

Yes. To update your phone number:

  1. Reply STOP from your old number to unsubscribe
  2. Register again with your new number at our home page

Or email raghustocks2020@gmail.com for assistance.

What is the sender's phone number?

Messages are sent from a Twilio toll-free number. The exact number may vary but will be consistent for your subscription. Always verify messages are related to trading alerts to avoid phishing.

Costs & Billing

Do you charge for this service?

No. This service is free. There are no subscription fees, registration fees, or hidden charges from us.

Will my carrier charge me?

Your mobile carrier may charge standard messaging rates based on your plan. Most modern plans include unlimited texting, but check with your carrier to be sure. We are not responsible for carrier charges.

How can I avoid carrier charges?

To avoid carrier charges:

  • Use a plan with unlimited texting (most common today)
  • Connect to WiFi if your phone supports WiFi texting
  • Monitor your usage with your carrier
