1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Raghuveer Ganta ("I", "me", or "my") collects, uses, and protects information in connection with the personal trading notification system (the "Service"). This Service provides automated SMS/text notifications for personal trading activities.

2. Information Collection

The Service collects and processes the following information:

  • Contact Information: Phone number and email address for notification delivery
  • Trading Data: Trade signals, positions, prices, and market data from connected platforms
  • System Data: Notification delivery status and system logs

Important: This is a personal notification system. All notifications are sent exclusively to my own phone number. No third-party data is collected or processed.

3. Use of Information

The collected information is used solely for:

  • Sending automated trading alerts and notifications via SMS
  • Synchronizing position data from trading platforms
  • Providing trade approval notifications
  • Delivering market screener alerts
  • System monitoring and troubleshooting

4. Information Sharing

I do not sell, trade, or share personal information with third parties, except as follows:

  • Service Providers: Twilio (for SMS delivery), ServiceNow (data management), and other necessary technical service providers
  • Trading Platforms: NinjaTrader, TradingView, IBKR TWS for receiving trading data
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect legal rights

5. SMS/Text Messaging

By using this Service, you consent to receive automated SMS notifications. Key details:

  • Frequency: Message frequency varies based on trading activity (typically 10-30 messages per day)
  • Content: Trade signals, position updates, market alerts, and trading summaries
  • Opt-Out: Reply STOP to any message to unsubscribe
  • Help: Reply HELP for assistance
  • Charges: Message and data rates may apply based on your mobile carrier plan

6. Data Security

I implement reasonable security measures to protect information:

  • HTTPS/SSL encryption for all web communications
  • Secure API authentication for platform integrations
  • Access controls on data storage systems
  • Regular security reviews and updates

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. I cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

7. Data Retention

Trading data and notification logs are retained for:

  • Active trading signals: 90 days
  • Historical position data: 1 year
  • System logs: 30 days

Data may be retained longer if required for legal or accounting purposes.

8. Third-Party Services

The Service integrates with the following third-party platforms:

  • Twilio: SMS message delivery (Privacy Policy: https://www.twilio.com/legal/privacy)
  • ServiceNow: Data management and workflow (Privacy Policy: https://www.servicenow.com/privacy-statement.html)
  • NinjaTrader: Futures trading platform
  • TradingView: Market analysis and alerts
  • Interactive Brokers: Position tracking

Each service has its own privacy policy governing the use of your information.

9. Personal Use Only

This is a personal notification system for individual trading activities. I do not:

  • Provide trading advice or recommendations to others
  • Sell or distribute trading signals
  • Operate as a registered investment advisor
  • Send notifications to third parties
