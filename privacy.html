Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 6, 2026

Important Notice: This Privacy Policy describes how Raghuveer Ganta ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our SMS trading alert service.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you register for our SMS alert service, we collect:

  • Phone Number: Your mobile phone number to send trading alerts
  • Consent Records: Documentation of your opt-in consent and timestamp
  • Subscription Status: Whether you are actively subscribed or have opted out

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Message delivery status and timestamps
  • Opt-in and opt-out events
  • HELP and STOP command responses

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect or store:

  • Financial account credentials or passwords
  • Social security numbers or government IDs
  • Credit card or payment information
  • Location data or device identifiers
  • Browsing history or cookies (this is a notification-only service)

2. How We Use Your Information

Your personal information is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Send Trading Alerts: Deliver SMS notifications about trade executions, position updates, and market signals
  • Service Management: Process opt-in and opt-out requests
  • Customer Support: Respond to your HELP requests and support inquiries
  • Compliance: Maintain records required by TCPA and carrier regulations
We Never Use Your Information For:
  • Marketing or promotional purposes
  • Selling or renting to third parties
  • Advertising or commercial solicitation
  • Any purpose other than sending trading alerts you consented to receive

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

3.1 SMS Opt-In Data Protection

Critical Protection: Mobile information, including opt-in data and consent records, will NOT be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. All categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

3.2 Service Providers

We use the following service providers who may process your phone number:

  • Twilio: SMS delivery platform - processes phone numbers only for message delivery under strict data protection agreements
  • Service providers are contractually prohibited from using your data for any other purpose

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, such as:

  • Complying with a subpoena or court order
  • Responding to lawful requests from government authorities
  • Protecting our legal rights or defending against claims

3.4 What We Never Share

  • Your phone number is never sold, rented, or shared with marketers
  • No third-party advertising or promotional use
  • No affiliate marketing or referral programs

4. Data Retention

We retain your information as follows:

  • Active Subscribers: Phone number and consent records maintained while subscribed
  • After Opt-Out: Records retained for 12 months for compliance purposes, then securely deleted
  • Message Logs: Delivery logs retained for 90 days for troubleshooting

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encrypted data transmission (TLS/SSL)
  • Secure storage systems with access controls
  • Regular security audits and monitoring
  • Limited employee access on need-to-know basis
